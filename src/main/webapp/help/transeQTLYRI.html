Transcript ratio quantitative trait loci (trQTL) for the Yoruba population samples.

For the transcript ratio QTLs (trQTLs), we sought to characterize the QTL effect on transcript usage. For each trQTL gene, we identified the transcript with highest association and the transcript with most negatively correlated quantifications to this. Given the annotation of these two transcripts we classified the events for each trQTL.
