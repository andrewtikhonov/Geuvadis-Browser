Exon expression quantitative trait loci (eQTL) for the European population samples.

Exon eQTLs in cis were calculated in a sample set of 373 Europeans by linear regression of genetic variants with >5% frequency in 1MB window from TSS and normalized exon quantifications. Only significant associations (5% FDR) are shown.

The bars represent genetic variants, with the height of the bar showing the p-value of eQTL association. The best variant of each gene is shown in grey. Details of each association can be seen by clicking the variant, and clicking on an associating exon on this will move the quantification track to the region of the exon.
